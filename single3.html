OFFICE DESIGN TRENDS TO WATCH IN 2019

Interconnectivity, sustainability, and technology are hallmarks of a “smart office”, a growing trend in workspace design. Smart offices have heralded a shift in design principles that parallel a change happening in organizational psychology circles. Today’s workforce is more “plugged in” than ever before, so easy access to day-to-day tech is key

However, designers and their clients are starting to place a greater emphasis on creating spaces that encourage collaboration, happiness, and productivity as well.
