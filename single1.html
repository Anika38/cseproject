Designing the cozy family kitchen

Decorating for fall to me is taking the cozy factor to the next level in every corner of the house. I also enjoy playing with different materials and different textures. This week I played around with some copper and fall elements in the kitchen & wanted to share this cozy fall kitchen corner with you…

For this corner of our kitchen, I incorporated fall tea towels, autumnal colors, and warm copper tones to bring together all the cozy fall vibes. I love that these are easy things to add or switch out in your kitchen … This takes minimal time and turns out super cute and cozy for fall! I love showing you guys that fall decor doesn’t have to be all bright orange … some of my favorites for fall are greens, amber bottles, copper pieces, autumnal patterns, neutrals, and white pumpkins..

I actually am dreaming about this same corner with a big colorful pumpkin, but alas it’s actually been really hard to find here in Michigan right now so I went with some dried gourds. The main inspiration for this corner of the kitchen? I picked up [this] tea towel from Anthropologie the other day along with [this] pot holder.

Anyways, I’ve been super focused on easy ways to transition our home into fall. Which is why when I tackled this corner I wanted to show you guys that simple elements can make any corner of your home super cozy.

Is anyone else loving really simple changes to transition your home into fall? In this season of life I am always switching things up but I really have been loving the way small changes can make a corner of your home so much cozier. I hope this inspires you and gives you ideas for making a corner of your home cozy for fall! If you decide to take on a corner of your home inspired by this post I’d love it if you tagged me on Instagram [HERE] so I can see your cozy corner. Thank you for visiting the blog today and everyday.
